More Infomation about each weapon effect and design

FireWall 
-was a basic Area of effect weapon damaging enemies whenever they enter within a certain radius around the player

YCircle360 (aka AntiVirus Shot)
- Shots projectiles Horizontialy from the player that is symmetrical on both sides with each projectiles being equal distance in angle from each other  

Force Quit Program (aka FQP)
- It selects a random n amount of enemeies with a higher weapon level selecting more enemies and killing them after a few seconds 	

Blast
- A simple lazar that shoots a lazar in the last direction the player was moving 

Queue Overflow
- Throws overflowing projectiles (aka files) upwards and the projectiles gets afffected by gavity falling slowing it down then falling downwards damaging enemies in its path

Binary Bomb
- Places bombs around the above the player similar to the visualation of a binary tree with the player being the root node in a similar look to the ASCII art below
